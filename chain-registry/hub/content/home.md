# Join the CREG testnet

The CREG Sepolia testnet (`creg-testnet-1`) is a coordinated lab for package publishers and validators. This hub is your starting point — explore both paths, connect a wallet when you are ready, and follow the guides to participate.

## Two ways to contribute

**Publish packages** — Ship signed packages to the registry, verify them on the explorer, and help stress-test the publisher workflow.

**Run a validator** — Stake test tokens, register on Sepolia, and operate a node using the operator runbook.

You do not need a wallet to read these guides. Connect when you want to preview your address or prepare for sign-in (coming in a later phase).

## Before you start

1. Get Sepolia ETH from the [faucet](https://faucet.testnet.cregnet.dev) for gas.
2. Use a test-only wallet — never your mainnet keys.
3. Switch your wallet to **Sepolia** (chain id `11155111`).

## What this hub is not

- Not the block explorer — use [explorer.testnet.cregnet.dev](https://explorer.testnet.cregnet.dev) for blocks, packages, and validators.
- Not the waitlist product — early access signup lives elsewhere.
- Not a place to paste private keys — enrollment uses links and docs only.
