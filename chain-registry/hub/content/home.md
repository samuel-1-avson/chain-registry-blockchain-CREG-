# Join the CREG Sepolia testnet

**creg-testnet-1** is a public lab for the Chain Registry on Ethereum Sepolia. Use it to publish signed packages, run validators, and explore how CREG coordinates releases on-chain.

You do not need a wallet to read these guides. Connect a wallet when you are ready for on-chain steps (faucet, stake, publish).

## Pick your path

- **Publish** — ship packages to the registry and verify them on the explorer.
- **Validate** — stake, register a node, and help secure the testnet.

Both paths use Sepolia ETH for gas. The hub links to the **faucet** and **explorer** when you need them.
